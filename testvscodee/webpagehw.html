Peyush Bansal (born 26 April 1985) is an Indian entrepreneur, business executive and an angel investor.
         He is the co-founder and CEO of Lenskart, an Indian multinational optical prescription eyewear retail chain, based in Gurugram. He is also a judge of the show Shark Tank India.


Achievements

Peyush Bansal - Awards & Achievements Some of his achievements are as follows: Red Herring Top 100 Asia Award 2013 Emerging Entrepreneur of the Year Award in 2012 British Honor's Award at McGill University Best 40 under 40 entrepreneurs 2021 Fortune India
